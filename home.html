What is religion?
The word "religion" comes from the Latin word religare , which means "to bind" and is related to the root of the English word "rule". Based on this, religion is not just a set of beliefs, but rather a set of beliefs that "bind" the believer.

The major world religions covered by this website (Buddhism, Christianity, Hinduism, Islam, Judaism, and Sikhism) differ in many aspects, such as their belief systems and how each religion is organized. There are many differences between them, but one thing they have in common is their adherence and faith in the tenets of their belief.

About Religiosity
The name "Religiosity" represents a combination of "Religion" and "Curiosity"- we want to provide a easy and helpful resource for people who are curious about religions.

We invite you to explore all that Religiosity has to offer- we have pages dedicated to each religion, different architectural styles of their places of worship, a map showing where each religion is practiced, and more! Satisfy your curiosity and learn something new!
