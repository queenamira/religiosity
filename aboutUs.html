Why we created Religiosity:

We created this website to help dispell false information about various religions and to have an easy way to learn about some of the major world religions. Today, there is so much false information and beliefs about other religions, causing violence and intolerance.
We wanted to combat this surge of intolerance by providing a website about six major religions in the world, in order to foster more understanding and awareness.
We hope that the information presented is accurate but take responsibility if it is not.
